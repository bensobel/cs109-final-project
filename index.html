Executive Summary

In this project, we sought to predict the price of an illiquid asset by applying machine-learning techniques to financial data.

The Data

Our project relied on data from Bloomberg, FactSet, the Center for Research in Security Prices (CRSP), Thomson Reuters Datastream, the Security and Exchange Commission's EDGAR database, and Google search. We used these sources to construct dataframes for the daily prices of over 29,000 traded securities from 1986 to the present day, as well as separate dataframes to track the prices of the illiquid assets on which our project focused: high-density polyethylene (HDPE) and low-density polyethylene (LDPE).
